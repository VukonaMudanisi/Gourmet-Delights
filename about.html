Our Story

Passion, flavor, and community – served fresh in the heart of Vanderbijlpark.

Welcome to Gourmet Delights

Established in 2018, Gourmet Delights was born from a love of food and a dream to bring world-class flavors to the Vaal Triangle. Located on SE7 Barrage Road, Vanderbijlpark, we pride ourselves on using only the freshest local ingredients, hand-picked daily.

From our crispy Vegetable Spring Rolls to the indulgent New York Cheesecake, every dish is crafted with care by our talented chefs.

Whether you're joining us for a family dinner, a quick lunch, or a special celebration, we promise a warm welcome and an unforgettable meal.

Our Values

  • Quality ingredients, every time
  • Friendly, attentive service
  • Supporting local farmers & suppliers
  • Eco-friendly practices (recyclable packaging)
